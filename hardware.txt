Relevant specs:
C (black) - 24 Vac neutral
G (green) - Fan control. Connect to R
Y (yellow) - Cooling control. Connect to RC
O/B (orange) - Connecting to R makes something click outside. Not sure what this actually does.
R (red) - 24 Vac power
RC (red) - 24 Vac power, specfically for cooling switch
