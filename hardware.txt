Relevant specs:
C (black) - 24 Vac neutral
G (green) - Fan control. Connect to R
Y (yellow) - Cooling control. Connect to RC
W (white) - Heating control. Connect to RH
O/B (orange) - 2nd stage cooling (unused here)
R (red) - 24 V ac power
RC (red) - 24 V ac power, specfically for cooling switch
RH (red) - 24 V ac power, specifically for heating switch
(All three power wires are noramally run in a series from one wire)
